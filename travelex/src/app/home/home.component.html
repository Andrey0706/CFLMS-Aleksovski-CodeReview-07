Ticketless travel is easy and convenient.

    So that you still have all the information about your trip, we will create a personal travel plan for you. This contains all relevant information about your booked trip, such as: B. the current departure times, the hotel address, catering services, rental car bookings etc. in a short overview. You will receive your travel documents about 4 weeks before departure via your MEINE account. The travel plan is primarily for information - it is sufficient for your trip to have a valid passport or ID card. Important: Please check your travel plan before departure for completeness and accuracy . In exceptional cases, individual documents (vouchers) for certain services must be printed out and carried with you on your trip. These are clearly marked with a "!" In your travel plan. Please print this out with the latest information shortly before your departure. In the event of changes to the trip (e.g. changed flight times, subsequently booked additional services, etc.), these documents (vouchers) must be reprinted, as the old ones lose their validity.
